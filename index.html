An architectural project typically involves designing a physical structure or space, such as a building, home, park, or public space. This process involves determining the client's needs and goals, analyzing the site's context and constraints, developing conceptual designs, creating detailed construction documents, and overseeing construction to ensure the project is built according to the design intent. The architect must balance aesthetic, functional, and technical considerations to create a safe, sustainable, and visually appealing space that meets the needs of its users.
